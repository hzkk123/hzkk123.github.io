About Me

I'm a Ph.D student from School of Aerospace, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) and pursing my doctor's degree in Mechanical Engineering. My research interest inludes Surrogate modeling, Bayesian optimization, robust design optimization, multi-objective optimization, and multi-fidelity surrogate. I am working on surrogate-based design optimization and learning with Prof. Qi Zhou. Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree from Zhengzhou University (ZZU), with a major in Mechanical Engineering.
